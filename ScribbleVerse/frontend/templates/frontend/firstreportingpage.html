ScribbleVerse

How to Play

  • 👨‍🎨 One player draws while others guess.
  • ⌨️ Type your guesses in the chat.
  • 🏆 Earn points for correct guesses.
  • 🎨 Use different colors and brush sizes.
  • 🚀 The player with the most points wins!
